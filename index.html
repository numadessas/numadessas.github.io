About

DIGITAL MATTER,
HUMAN TOUCH.

I'm Manuella Godoy, also know as Numa — a designer, artist, researcher, and educator based in Brazil. My work sits at the intersection of emerging technologies and cultural practice, with a particular focus on blockchain ecosystems and multimedia applications.

On the practice side, I work with visual communication, branding, digital products, creative direction, webdesign, audiovisual production, illustration, and 3D modeling. I build visual identities, soundtracks, videos, and diverse digital experiences.

I also develop educational content, workshops, and courses on blockchain for different levels of familiarity — from beginners to professionals seeking to deepen their knowledge.
